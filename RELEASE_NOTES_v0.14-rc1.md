# Gurbani Reader v0.14 RC 1

This is an installable local Android test build. It is not an App Store or Play Store release.

## Find Gurbani

- Roman search now uses a normalised and phonetic offline index. Searches such as `jo mange`, `jo mangai`, `naam`, `sukhmani` and common Bani title variants are supported without changing keyboard.
- Search suggestions update while typing and open a Bani, contributor, Raag or matching Shabad directly.
- Push-to-speak search supports Punjabi and Roman/English recognition on Android. The transcript remains editable and alternative recognitions can be selected.
- Search history is separate from reading history and supports deleting one entry or clearing either history.
- Complete Shabads include an external “Find a recording on YouTube” action.

## Browse and daily reading

- Browse begins with Daily Prayers, Common Banis, Ceremonies, Raags, Contributors and Research rather than database-shaped categories.
- Kirtan Sohila is now installed as the complete 55-line BaniDB reading and included in Daily Prayers.
- Sukhmani Sahib, Asa Di Vaar, Salok Mahalla 9 and other familiar readings are surfaced under Common Banis with common spelling aliases.
- Principal Raags follow their Guru Granth Sahib sequence. Structural headings such as Chaubolas are separated.
- Laavan is nested within the Anand Sanskar sequence.

## Calmer interface and clearer sources

- Mobile navigation is reduced to Home, Search, Browse and Library; reading screens use an immersive layout with in-app Back.
- The compact Home row combines Continue Reading with the Ang control.
- A first-run guide and Sources page explain BaniDB and SikhRI’s Guru Granth Sahib Project in plain language.
- SikhRI and BaniDB translations have visible provider labels, contrasting backgrounds and solid-versus-dashed borders.
- Indigo, Burgundy, Slate and Forest accent palettes can be selected in Library settings.

Text and baseline translations are from BaniDB. The Guru Granth Sahib Project material is from Sikh Research Institute and is used with permission.
