# Gurbani Reader v0.9 alpha 1

First usability-redesign checkpoint built on the tagged `v0.8.0` corpus release.

## Implemented

- Authors now open paginated lists of their BaniDB Shabad groups.
- Author, search, exact-word and Ang results open a complete Shabad view.
- Ang reading and Shabad reading are separate modes.
- SikhRI/TGGSP layers are displayed once per mapped Shabad and labelled by content type and language.
- Word tapping is disabled during ordinary reading and available through an explicit word-exploration mode.
- Independent decrease, reset and increase controls for Gurmukhi, transliteration and interpretation.
- Adjustable line spacing.
- Gurmukhi search, phonetic Latin variants (`nam`, `naam`, `nām`) and mapped SikhRI/TGGSP English-content search.
- Search results are grouped at Shabad level.
- Exact concordance is paginated in batches of 50 with no 200-result ceiling.

## Still scheduled for later v0.9 checkpoints

- Curated optional frequency families with explicit included forms.
- Raag and named-Bani browsers.
- TGGSP mapped/ambiguous/unmatched coverage review browser.
- Search ranking refinement and Android-device performance testing.
