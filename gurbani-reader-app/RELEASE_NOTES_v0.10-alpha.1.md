# Gurbani Reader v0.10 alpha 1

This is the navigation, source-scoping and reading-interface release.

## Included

- Android Back returns to the previous in-app screen.
- Reading mode provides uninterrupted full-Ang and full-Sabad reading; Study mode exposes line tools.
- Browse by named Bani, every contributor, Raag and the complete ranked word index.
- Contributor and Raag views report Sabad counts, with contributor filtering inside a Raag.
- Search supports Gurmukhi, phonetic roman spellings, TGGSP English, Bani titles, contributors, Raags and indexed first letters.
- Experimental theme search is explicitly limited to keyword matches in available TGGSP English analysis.
- Ten independently selectable TGGSP sections, with one compact attribution per reader view.
- Guru Granth Sahib and the combined Vaaran Bhai Gurdas collection are available offline. Bhai Gurdas Ji and Bhai Gurdas Singh Ji remain identifiable as contributors.
- Paper, sepia, light, dark and black themes; independent Gurmukhi and Latin/English sizes and colours.

## Data boundaries

Canonical text and indexing are derived from the attributed BaniDB snapshot. TGGSP material is stored as separately typed, attributed layers and never attached to a Sabad by an unreviewed guess. Named Bani ordering is a separate attributed BaniDB layer.
