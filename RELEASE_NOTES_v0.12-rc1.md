# Gurbani Reader v0.12 RC 1

This is an installable local Android test build. It is not an App Store or Play Store release.

## Main changes

- Home prioritises Search, Banis and Go to Ang; its modules can be reordered or hidden.
- Search is live while typing, defaults visibly to All texts, retains the compact `ਕ` Gurmukhi keyboard, and can filter TGGSP translation, word analysis, or extended material.
- Dictionary supports Gurmukhi and Roman spellings. English concepts can suggest several Gurbani terms and are explicitly marked Experimental.
- The reader resumes the last Ang and uses compact `Aa` and `Layers` controls.
- TGGSP transliteration, literal translation, meanings/etymology, extended sections and English/Panjabi display can be controlled independently.
- Passage translations are displayed after the whole supplied Gurbani block and labelled as passage-level content.
- Asa Di Vaar is the preferred display name; Asa Ki Vaar, Aasa and Var variants remain searchable aliases.
- Android back gestures use in-app history and remain in the app at Home.

The source text is from BaniDB. SikhRI’s The Guru Granth Sahib Project material is used with permission and attributed in the app.
